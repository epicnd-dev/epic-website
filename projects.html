Projects

Spring 2021

Gas Turbine Performance

GE Power

Energy

MacKenzie Cavanagh

Insider Threat Analyst

Work with GE Power to create a model of gas turbine performance that predicts energy yield, Carbon emissions, and Nitrogen oxide emissions. members of the team will Use data science and python to build a machine learning model that applies the theory of thermodynamics.

Project goals include Understanding how a Combined Cycle Power Plant works, Developing models to predict energy output and harmful emissions, and determining if it is possible for such power plants to be carbon neutral.

the project Begins early March and lasts through spring semester. members are required to commit 3-5 hours per week as they work on an interdisciplinary team of engineers.
